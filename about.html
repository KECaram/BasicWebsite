About the Artist

My name is Keith Caram, and I guess this is a fan website. Marla is my sister and this is a website that I am making that is dedicated to her art and performances. She is kind and passionate and a really great sister, so this is a little thing that I made to show my pride in her work. Not all of the information on the site is true, but all of the art featured on this website is her art. She has been an artist as long as I can remember and has always made the most amazing art, especailly when featuring her family and cats. She recently moved back to MA from NYC and I miss her dearly. I hope you share in some of the joy that her art has given me!
